About Us

Our Mission & Vision

At Columbus Model High School, our mission is to provide a stimulating learning environment with a technological orientation across the whole curriculum, which maximizes individual potential and ensures students of all ability levels are well equipped to meet the challenges of education, work and life.

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school: respect, tolerance & inclusion, and excellence.

Principal's Message

"Welcome to Columbus Model High School! We are dedicated to fostering an environment where every student can achieve their full potential. Our committed staff, comprehensive programs, and vibrant community work together to create an enriching educational experience. We believe in 'Sparking Growth and Joy' in every child's learning journey."

- Hima Bindu

Our Academic Programs

Primary Years

Building a Strong Foundation for Lifelong Learning.
Our Primary Years program is designed to spark curiosity and a love for learning. We focus on building essential literacy and numeracy skills through a blend of structured lessons and inquiry-based exploration. In our nurturing classrooms, students develop crucial social skills, creativity, and confidence, ensuring a joyful and positive start to their educational journey.

Learn More →

Middle School

Navigating Adolescence with Confidence and Skill.
The Middle School years are a critical time for personal and academic growth. Our curriculum challenges students to think critically, work collaboratively, and become more independent learners. We introduce a wider range of specialized subjects and emphasize project-based learning to connect classroom concepts to the real world. Our supportive environment helps students build resilience and a strong sense of responsibility as they prepare for the rigors of High School.

Learn More →

High School

Preparing for a Future of Limitless Possibilities.
Our High School program is designed to prepare students for success in college, careers, and life. We offer a rigorous academic curriculum with diverse elective choices, including Advanced Placement (AP) courses, that allow students to pursue their passions. We focus on developing leadership skills, fostering intellectual curiosity, and encouraging community involvement. Graduates from Columbus Model High School are well-rounded, confident individuals ready to make a positive impact on the world.

Admissions

Join Our Community

We welcome aspiring students to become a part of the Columbus Model High School family. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and supportive.

  • Fill out the online application form.
  • Please bring candidate Aadhaar, birth certificate and parents address proof.
  • Submit the completed form along with required documents.
  • Pay the application fee (if applicable).
  • Appear for an interaction session/entrance test (as applicable).
  • Receive admission confirmation and complete enrollment.
