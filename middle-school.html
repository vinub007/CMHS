Developing Critical Thinkers and Confident Leaders

Our Middle School program challenges students to grow academically and personally as they prepare for high school.

Academic Excellence

Core Curriculum

Students engage in advanced topics in Math (Pre-Algebra/Algebra), Literature, History, and Sciences, developing strong analytical and critical thinking skills.

Electives & Exploration

A wide range of electives, including foreign languages, public speaking, robotics, and digital media, allows students to explore their interests and discover new passions.

Project-Based Learning

Signature projects like our annual Science Expo and History Fair challenge students to apply classroom knowledge to real-world problems, fostering collaboration and creativity.

Support for Every Student

Our advisory program pairs small groups of students with a dedicated faculty advisor for mentorship, academic guidance, and personal support, ensuring every child feels known and valued.

Beyond the Classroom

From competitive sports to academic clubs, our extracurricular activities help students build teamwork, leadership, and a strong sense of community.

Athletics (Soccer, Basketball) Debate Club Student Government Community Service
