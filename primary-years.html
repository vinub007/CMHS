Nurturing Curiosity, Building Foundations

Our Primary Years Program sparks a lifelong love for learning in a supportive and engaging environment.

A Rich & Balanced Curriculum

Core Subjects

We focus on building essential literacy and numeracy skills through a blend of structured lessons and inquiry-based exploration in Math, Language Arts, Science, and Social Studies.

Specialist Subjects

Students' creativity and physical development are nurtured through specialist classes in Art, Music, Physical Education, and basic technology integration.

Assessment Philosophy

We track progress through meaningful methods like observation, portfolios, and regular parent-teacher conferences, emphasizing individual growth and confidence over grades.
